Lenna is passionate about investigating what emerging technologies can do for the architectural profession. She earned her Masters in Architecture from Florida International University where her studies explored interactive design aided by sensors, actuators, and computing. In 2018, Lenna was invited to attend the Cyborg Digital Futures workshop in Shanghai, led by Philip Yuan. There she explored the use of emerging technology to create a pneumatic suit that promotes blood circulation throughout the body. Lenna graduated top of her class as a Tau Sigma Delta member and played basketball at the collegiate level in her Undergraduate Studies at Judson University.

In her free time you can find Lenna 3-D printing, investigating interactive design, and searching for her lunch.
